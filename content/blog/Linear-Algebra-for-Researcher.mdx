```math
\vec{u} = \begin{pmatrix} 2 \\ -1 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix}
\quad \text{and} \quad
c\vec{u} = c\begin{pmatrix} 2 \\ -1 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix}
= \begin{pmatrix} 2c \\ -c \\ 3c \end{pmatrix}.
```

Their sum is computed componentwise:
